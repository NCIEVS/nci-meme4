Source Insertion Overview

Instructor

Brian Carlsen

Goals

  • What you should learn:
    • The three forms a "recipe" takes
    • Where test and real insertions take place, and what files are involved
    • Timing of test and real insertions
    • Why each of the five conceptual sections of a recipe are important
    • Why insertions cannot be run during editing
  • What you should be able to do:
    • Explain why we perform test insertions.
