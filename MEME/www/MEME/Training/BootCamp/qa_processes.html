Goals

  • What you should learn:
    • Understand the MRD and MID QA processes
    • Understand the workflow to resolve QA discrepancies
    • Understand the procedure to investigate MRD and MID QA results
  • What you should be able to do:
    • Run MRD and MID QA procedures
    • Run auto fix, edit check or adjustment for the MID
    • Edit MRD QA report
