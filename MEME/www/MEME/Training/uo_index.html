Overview/Objective

This is the index page for a series of training documents which analyze the Metathesaurus life-cycle and associated tasks from the perspective of the various UMLS Objects.  We explain the form of data this object takes (file, table, object, etc) at each stage of processing, and how it is treated in the code, in the documentation, and by the quality assurance processes.
