Table name:rel_directionality
Description:Contains long/short forms of rel names. This should really be subsumed into inverse_rel_attributes.
Fields:
short_form VARCHAR2(20) The short form of a relationship name. Must be in inverse_relationships. 
long_form VARCHAR2(150) The long form of a relationship name. 
Indexes: No indexes
