aui_history

Overview/Objective

This document contains a description of aui_history and its various fields. It also (may) include information about how this table is used in the various stages of MID processing.
 

Details

Table name:aui_history
Table Type: auxiliary
Description:Tracks history of AUI movement (birth,death,splits, etc).
Fields:
map_id NUMBER(12)  
aui1 VARCHAR2(10)  
cui1 VARCHAR2(10) a Concept Unique Identifier, e.g. C0000039 
ver VARCHAR2(50) a version of the Metathesaurus, e.g. 2001AA 
relationship_name VARCHAR2(10) must be in inverse_relationships.relationship_name 
relationship_attribute VARCHAR2(100) must be in inverse_rel_attributes.relationship_attribute 
map_reason VARCHAR2(4000) an explanation of why this CUI was mapped, if it was mapped 
aui2 VARCHAR2(10)  
cui2 VARCHAR2(10) a Concept Unique Identifier, e.g. C0000039 
authority VARCHAR2(50) the authority responsible for this row, can be validated by MEME_RANKS.get_source_authority_rank 
timestamp DATE indicates when this row was inserted or last modified 
Indexes:
  • x_aui_history ON aui1
