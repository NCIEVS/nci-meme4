Tracking Concepts Approved

This plot shows the number of concepts approved by day over the past 'n' months. Concepts are approved by editors either manually using the interface or by a batch process known as "stamping" run on behalf of the editor who had responsibility for that worklist. The plot can be selected for either or both types of approval.

Please don't place too much reliance on data prior to late March 1998 as MEME-II was still in its testing phase then.
