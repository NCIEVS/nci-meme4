Tracking Concept Status

The concept_status table maintains the status of all the concepts in the MID. A status of R means the concept has been reviewed, N means the concept needs review by an editor, a U indicates the concept is unreviewed and will not be edited (e.g., MeSH supplementary chemicals), and an E stands for embryo concepts that have not yet had all their attributes and/or class memberships assigned.

Submitting the following form will show a frequency plot of these values over time. The plot shows R concepts in red, N in blue, U in green, and E in cyan.

As new sources are inserted and new concepts merged with existing ones, the status of the latter will change from R to N. As concepts pass through the editorial process, their status changes from N to R. E concepts should only be present around an insertion.

Data has been collected daily using the following SQL and is available in the table: ems_concept_status_count. 

select status, count(*) from concept_status group by status\g
