Tracking Concepts Approved

This plot shows the number of concepts approved daily over the past 'n' months. Concepts are approved by editors either manually using the interface or by a batch process known as "stamping" run on behalf of the editor who had responsibility for that worklist.

The data is collected daily in the table: ems_edit_count.
