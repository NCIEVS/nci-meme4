ICD10AM Editing Tips

  • ICD10AM contains mostly diseases or procedures. There are a few exceptions based on code. The paragraph below is from the ICD10AM documentation and explains what X, Y and Z codes mean.
External Causes of Morbidity and Mortality

 Y93 Activity 
     Note: The following category is for use with categories 
           V01-Y43 to identify the activity of the injured
           person at the time the event occurred.
           
           This category should not be confused with, or be used 
           instead of, category Y92 Place of Occurrence which is 
           provided to indicate the place of occurrence of the event 
           at the time the injury occurred.
